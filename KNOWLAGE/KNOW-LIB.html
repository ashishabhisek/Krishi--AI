Pest & Disease Management

Learn to identify and control common pests and diseases affecting your crops

Brown Plant Hopper

Affects: Rice, Wheat

Small brown insects that suck plant sap, causing yellowing and stunted growth.

Control Methods:

  • Use resistant varieties
  • Apply neem-based insecticides
  • Maintain proper water management

Leaf Blight

Affects: Rice, Maize

Fungal disease causing brown spots on leaves, leading to reduced photosynthesis.

Prevention & Control:

  • Use certified disease-free seeds
  • Apply fungicides as preventive measure
  • Ensure proper field drainage

Cotton Bollworm

Affects: Cotton, Tomato

Caterpillars that feed on flowers, fruits, and bolls, causing significant yield loss.

Management Strategy:

  • Use pheromone traps
  • Release beneficial insects
  • Apply Bt-based sprays
